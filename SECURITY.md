## Labelling an update

Updates Should be named on the below given basis

2x2 
* *2x2 Beg OLL* - Beginner OLL
* *2x2 Beg PLL* - Beginner PLL
* *2x2 Int Ortega* - Intermediate OLL
* *2x2 Mas Full EG* - Advanced CLL, Master EG 1, Master EG 2
